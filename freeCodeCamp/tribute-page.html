Here is a short summary of Stevie Wonder's life:


  • 1950: Born Stevland Hardaway Jenkins in May 13, 1950, in Saginaw, Michigan
  • 1963: Stevie's single, Fingertips, became a major hit
  • 1970: Wonder married Syreeta Wright and released Where I'm Coming From
  • 1972: Wonder released Talking Book, which featured the hit singles Superstition and You are the Sunshine of My Life, which both won two Grammies
  • 1976: Wonder released Songs in the Key of Life
  • 1979: Wonder released Stevie Wonder's Journey Through "The Secret Life of Plants, which featured extensive use of electronic sampler
  • 1987: Wonder was in a featured duet with Bruce Springsteen for an African Famine Relief charity concert
  • 1996: Wonder sang at the Summer Olympics closing ceremony
  • 2009: Wonder sang at the Michael Jackson memorial service
